ORDERING

How do I order?
Order through this link or DM us on instagram!
We will contact you regarding payment and collection details once you have placed your order.

Email us for larger coperate/event orders.
How long in advance do I need to place my order?
At least 2 days in advance.
If you need an order urgently, do reach out and we will try our best to accomodate! :)
Do you have cakes/cookies in other sizes or quantities?
The size of our standard bakes are as reflected on our products page and our order form.

For alternative sizes, please email us to check on the availability.
Cooperate orders are welcomed!
Can I order it as a gift/suprise for someone else?
Definately, we provide a hand written note for the receiver too!
However, please ensure that the receiver is at the location to receive the bakes.
In the event nobody is present to receive the bakes, a re-delivery fee of $10 applies.
What if I need to cancel my order?
Cancel more than a week before the delivery date: 100% refund
Cancel 4 days to a week before the delivery date: 50% refund
Cancel less than 4 days before the delivery date: no refund

CAKE AND COOKIE CARE

How should I store the bakes?
All our bakes are best eaten on the day of delivery for the best taste and texture.

Cookies: Store in our air tight container. Place at room temp. Consume within 10 days.

Cakes: Store in the refrigerator. Consume within 5 days
